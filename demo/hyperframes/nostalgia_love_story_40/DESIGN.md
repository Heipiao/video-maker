# Nostalgia Love Story 40

## Style Prompt

Warm editorial wedding memory film with cream paper texture, soft grain, gentle gold light, and romantic serif titles. The video should feel like a polished wedding teaser assembled from intimate documentary photos, not a loud social template.

## Colors

- Paper: `#F4E8D8`
- Ink: `#3B2A22`
- Rose: `#B8756F`
- Gold: `#C6A15B`
- Shadow: `#16110D`

## Typography

- Display: `Georgia`, serif fallback
- Body: `Inter`, system sans fallback

## Motion

- Slow parallax zoom on photos.
- Gentle upward text entrances.
- Light leak and soft paper fades between scenes.
- No harsh cuts, neon colors, or fast glitch effects.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic blue/purple gradients.
- Do not crowd text over faces.
- Do not use cartoon wedding elements.
- Do not rely on tiny captions for emotional beats.
